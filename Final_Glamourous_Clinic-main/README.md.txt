This project is designed to assist clinics by offering innovative features that streamline clinic operations. Developed using C#, .NET, and SQL Server, it aims to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of clinic management. These technologies work together to provide a robust and scalable solution for handling various aspects of clinic workflows, from patient scheduling and record-keeping to billing and reporting. By leveraging these tools, clinics can improve their service delivery, reduce administrative burdens, and ensure a higher quality of patient care.
